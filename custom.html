custom shoes/mugs

I am a fan of statement pieces, and I like to create DIY clothing in order to express myself. Every year, I create personalized designs on a pair of shoes to highlight an aspect of my personality, making a new pair whenever the old one is worn out.

I also like giving out custom-made gifts. One summer, I created custom mugs as presents for three of my friends and for my little brother. The mugs were designed with each person in mind, so the styles varied slightly.
